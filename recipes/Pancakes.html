Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

brief overview

Perfect pancakes are easier to make than you think. This pancake recipe produces thick, fluffy,
and all-around delicious pancakes with just a few ingredients that are probably already in your kitchen (and it's so much better than the boxed stuff).
